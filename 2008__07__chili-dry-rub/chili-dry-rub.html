This dry rub uses both ancho and guajillo chili and is great for chicken or pork.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Tbs Ancho Chili Powder
  • 1 Dried Guajillo Chili Pepper, stem removed
  • 1 Tbs Ground Cumin
  • 1 Tbs Light Brown Sugar
  • 1 Tbs Whole Coriander Seed
  • 1/2 TspÂ  Ground Cinnamon
  • 1 Tsp Kosher Salt
  • 1/2 Tbs Whole Peppercorns

Toss everything into a small food processor and pulse until well blended.
